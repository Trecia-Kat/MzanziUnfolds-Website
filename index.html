About Us

A Platform To Find Only Local Products

MzanziUnfolds.Media is a platform thats allows its users to search or discover local South African content. The content targets Instagram pages, YouTube channels, Twitter threads, accounts, videos and potentially Twitter spaces. Both admins and content creators/owners are allowed to add content of their own on the platform. Let's start creating Mzanzi!
